# 05 — User Stories: The Ecosystem in Motion (Rethought & Expanded)

> **RETHINKING-Fable edition** — thirteen narrative scenarios: the five originals rewritten against the upgraded models, plus eight new compound stories exercising the synergies in [`02-SHARED-PRIMITIVES-AND-SYNERGY-MATRIX.md`](02-SHARED-PRIMITIVES-AND-SYNERGY-MATRIX.md). Each story imports as a `UserStory` node with `demonstrates →` edges ([`04`](04-GRAPH-SCHEMA-AND-EDGE-CATALOG.md) §1.2). Two invariants hold in every story: **the human stays the executive** (agents stage; autonomy graduates per-task, never globally), and **confidence stays visible** (every derived thing shows how sure it is and why).

---

## US-01 · The Fan-Out (rewritten)
Evening. To the kitchen device, or typed into Divia.Life: *"I had El Pollo Loco for dinner."* The sentence lands as one immutable **Fact** in the Activity Log. On the next agent pass, interpretations attach as edges: a food event (quesadillas ×2 → pantry decrement, cascading through the recipe model), a nutrition intake (~1,050 kcal, banded), an estimated dining expense (merchant resolved, amount inferred from *this user's* priors with a confidence band), a place visit. Subscribed apps render their interpretation; nothing is copied. Later the card charge posts — $11.80 — and *fuses* with the estimate: one interpretation confirmed, its confidence snapping to 1.0 everywhere at once, because there was only ever one fact. The user's single tap on the recap card (a Divia.Life Journal DiviaCard, not an email) confirms the batch; each correction sharpens next week's priors.
**Status:** capture surfaces real/in-build; interpretation layer v2. **Demonstrates:** P-01, P-02, P-12, ambient fusion (S-01).

## US-02 · The Nightly Replenishment (rewritten)
Every night after the household goes quiet, the DiviaHome agent reviews consumption. The pantry is a confidence-aware ledger of atoms — the milk estimate says ~⅓ carton ± a glass, burn rate two days. So: a **time-decaying grocery item** (needed within 48h, escalating nightly) and a bump to the grocery-errand's priority, staged, never ordered — unless milk was long ago graduated to auto-reorder, in which case the order goes out with an audit line in the morning packet. By breakfast the shopping list has quietly updated and the errand has climbed the morning plan on its own. Nobody filled out a form; the system watched, inferred within stated confidence, and staged the right action at the right time.
**Status:** aspirational; DiviaHome pre-code; the loop's data model (TastyPantry) designed. **Demonstrates:** P-03, P-05, R-11 repair, the nightly-cadence argument.

## US-03 · The GTD Weekly Review (rewritten)
All week, captures funneled into the Inboxes from every surface. Ahead of Sunday's session the agent runs the eleven-step first pass: pre-clarified captures, open loops surfaced from logged calls ("you told Sarah you'd send the contract — no task exists"), done-detection, waiting-for aging, stalled-project flags, someday-items whose stated preconditions came true, three creative provocations mined from the whole graph. The packet: *"14 items clarified, 3 stalled projects, 2 aged waiting-fors, 3 prompts. ~20 minutes."* The human confirms and decides; the agent never completes, deletes, or commits — GTD's psychological payoff *is* the human renegotiating their own commitments, so the clerk/executive boundary is the feature, not a limitation. Quarterly, the agent reviews itself: "you accepted 71% of drafted next-actions; rejections cluster around family commitments" — and recalibrates.
**Status:** aspirational; fully specified. **Demonstrates:** P-05, P-12, review-the-reviewer, cross-app Get-Creative.

## US-04 · The Cross-Timezone Dev Team (rewritten)
A feature decomposes on the platform; sub-tasks land with the agents suited to them — the architect shapes the approach, the cross-vendor guardian queues to review, the documentarian will update the docs. On the laptop, a CollabPair opens for the tricky part: one agent proposes, the human steers, a different-vendor agent critiques — deterministic gates (tests, lint, the approved-plan check) arbitrate, not rhetoric. The whole session lands byte-for-byte in the archive. Dallas checkpoints at 5pm; Bangalore resumes at 9am with full context — the agents carry the continuity. Months later the platform flags a security pattern from a FinTech project recurring on an IoT project: not agent memory alone but the *mined archive* — institutional knowledge that no longer walks out the door.
**Status:** CollabPairs + archive shipping; platform-scale coordination gated on server E2E. **Demonstrates:** P-05, P-10, S-10, the hybrid engine.

## US-05 · Federated Home and Work (rewritten)
One identity authenticates against the reference IdP; the phone flips between *work* (the employer's Enterprise tenancy) and *home* (the self-hosted DiviaHome) as a context switch, not a re-login. The servers stay separately owned; Global knows *who you are*, never *what you do* — the audit trail lives on infrastructure you control. Agents inherit the boundary: household agents hold household credentials at household autonomy grades; work agents hold work scopes (P-09) — the federation boundary is the agent-authority boundary by construction. Cross-context queries ("can I take on the school fundraiser this month?") execute only under an explicit per-query grant. The employer chose Divia identity; the fintech next door speaks the same open spec with its own IdP — federation is a protocol capability, not a suite membership.
**Status:** aspirational; placeholder identity fields already carried (hook #2). **Demonstrates:** P-09, R-03 repair, privacy-as-architecture.

## US-06 · The Morning Briefing (new)
7:04am. The kitchen device, unprompted but on schedule: *"Three things low — milk's on today's list. The ledger flagged one anomaly: the streaming service billed twice. Your review packet is ready for Sunday. And the school fundraiser thread got a reply."* On the phone, the same fan-in as a card stack; at the office, the Enterprise homepage renders the work skin — agent-fleet status, two approvals pending, one world-event on the team's watch-list. Every card deep-links to its staging surface: the briefing *is* the approval queue. One pipeline, three skins, and the whole ecosystem becomes something a person *feels* once a day instead of twelve notification streams.
**Status:** aspirational; assembled entirely from primitives already specified. **Demonstrates:** S-15, P-01 fan-in, the Agent NOC's consumer face.

## US-07 · The Dinner Question (new)
5:50pm: *"I'm hungry — what are my options?"* The answer draws five sources through one consented query: the pantry (what's on hand, with confidence — "probably enough chicken"), the palate graph (smoky-citrus scores high; you're two weeks into re-introducing cilantro), the health record (your CGM history dislikes late heavy carbs; tonight's earlier walk buys headroom), the ledger ("$31 comfortably spendable tonight"), and the Agenda (73 minutes before the call). Three ranked answers: a 25-minute pantry recipe with a one-jar shopping gap; the taco place that fits budget and glucose history; and a "stretch" blend suggestion that advances the cumin bridge you've been exploring. Choosing the recipe decrements inventory, logs macros, and stages the jar onto tomorrow's list.
**Status:** aspirational; every component individually specified. **Demonstrates:** S-08, the compound query as the ecosystem's consumer proof.

## US-08 · The Regulatory Radar Day (new)
3:12am: the nightly sweep diffs yesterday's statutes, agency actions, and dockets against CrowdMadness's compliance-posture graph. One hit: a midwestern state's AG issued sweepstakes guidance that plausibly reaches prize-tier mechanics. By 3:40 the agent has staged: a required-change diff (geofence the prize tier in that state; leaderboard mode unaffected), the evidence chain (guidance text, the two analogous precedents, confidence: medium-high), and a one-page brief. 9am: the human reviews, approves the geofence (an L2 gated execution — config change, deterministic gate, audit line), and forwards the brief to counsel for the medium-confidence question. Blast-radius check: the same guidance touches nothing else in the portfolio — the graph checked, automatically. Cost of the night's compliance department: about four dollars.
**Status:** buildable NOW ([`01`](01-TECHNOLOGY-HORIZON-2026-2029.md) §1); the venture's founding capability. **Demonstrates:** P-14, S-05, the autonomy ladder in a regulated domain.

## US-09 · The Remodel (new)
A homeowner lands on a ReDreamRebuild flooring guide (the $1.49-vs-$2.99-vs-$6 teaching), then goes deeper: the tier explorer shows the spectrum side-by-side with live prices; a standing watch catches the discontinued color at $1.99 across three regional stores. The contractor shortlist runs through safety report-cards — one candidate's clean two-year record turns out, via successor-entity detection, to continue a dissolved LLC with eleven violations; the report-card says so, with public-record citations and a correction channel. During the build, an independent reviewer signs photo-documented milestone receipts; the multimodal assistant flags the roof-tile layering in Tuesday's photos as a candidate defect — caught before the felt goes on, exactly the failure the founder's friend once caught by eye. The project budget lives as a runway in the ledger. At listing time, the before/after 3D captures and the Renovation-ROI packet (informed-choice delta, made visible) anchor the price. The $910K outcome, systematized.
**Status:** aspirational composite; each component NEAR-band. **Demonstrates:** S-06, S-07, S-11, P-06 adversarial, the content→data→tools ladder.

## US-10 · The Fractional Purchase (new)
A buyer opens an EstimatePacket for a Manhattan Beach ⅛-share: whole-home range, pro-rata arithmetic, modeled share fair value *with the bridge visible* — turnkey premium, calendar value, reserve adequacy, a view-durability scenario (the neighboring lot's legal-max envelope could take the ocean line: shown as labeled bands, not a fake dollar coefficient), liquidity notes, and two lanes marked "insufficient evidence — human review ordered." The co-purchase forms; purchase flows, escrow mechanics, and the operating ledger run on delegated LegendaryMoney rails; the reserve fund is a runway with escalating contribution nudges. Every governance vote and disbursement seals as a signed receipt attached to the share. Three years later, at resale, the share carries its own verifiable history — governance, reserves, use, maintenance — and prices *against its record*, not against marketing arithmetic. The 8× trap dies by daylight.
**Status:** aspirational; v1 = the manual-validated packet. **Demonstrates:** S-11, S-12, P-02 bands, P-03 reserves, honest-positioning as product.

## US-11 · The Monday Partners' Brief (new)
Sunday night, the dreaming run: watch-list events from the world graph (a portfolio company's competitor raised; a CA ADU statute amendment passed), link-prediction proposals (two question-mark edges: the statute event → the shelved ADU-advisory idea; a new import → nearest-neighbor cluster at ReDreamRebuild), and one Triangulation-Target re-evaluation — the ADU idea's feasibility distance just collapsed; the model recommends pulling it from far-band toward a current Build-Line conversation. Monday 8am, the partners open the brief — a LiveDocument, evidence-linked, with each proposal awaiting confirm/reject/defer. Two GPs confirm the ADU edge; the idea moves to Module-2 discussion with its decade of accumulated research attached. The MobThought miss, structurally impossible to repeat: the watcher exists now.
**Status:** the KSVGPS v1 target sequence ([`VENTURES/KingmakerStrategic-KSVGPS.md`](VENTURES/KingmakerStrategic-KSVGPS.md) §3). **Demonstrates:** S-03, S-13, P-07, P-13, the feasibility radar.

## US-12 · A World Event Ripples (new)
Tuesday, 11am: a major packaged-food recall breaks. The world graph resolves it to entities within minutes; credibility scoring confirms multi-source. The ripples, each inside its own sovereign graph, each under its own consent: the household's pantry flags two on-hand items (batch codes matched from receipt data) and stages a return-and-replace errand; the ledger annotates the original purchases and drafts the refund claims; the health record notes the exposure window against the family's symptom log — hypothesis-framed; a KSVGPS watch-list pings — a portfolio company supplies packaging to the recalled brand: exposure brief staged for the partners; and on CrowdMadness, the open market on the manufacturer's quarterly guidance re-prices as informed forecasters move, evidence chain attached for eventual resolution. One public fact; five private meanings; zero private data pooled anywhere.
**Status:** aspirational; the watch-list service (S-03) is the enabling piece. **Demonstrates:** the Four-Graphs architecture end-to-end, consent surfaces, P-01 at world scale.

## US-13 · The New Venture Bootstrap (new)
A GP voice-notes an idea on the drive home. By morning it's a Layer-A node: embedded, clustered (nearest topic: e-commerce fulfillment; nearest dormant asset: Invendra — flagged, with its held domains and the old musing attached), deduplicated against the ledger, and enriched by the overnight run with three watch-list events and a first competitive sketch. Weeks later it graduates: the blueprint executes — sub-LLC under the Series fund (LegalEvent node; the QSBS clock will start at the later C-corp reincorporation, runway attached), IP assigned from the repository (domains re-pointed, assignment receipt signed), repo instantiated from the canonical template, Build-Line and Envelope declared in the dev graph, and the venture's compliance posture subscribed to the radar. Every step that was once a week of remembering is a staged checklist a human approves in an afternoon — and if the venture ever pauses, everything detaches back to Layer A intact, wiser by one arc.
**Status:** aspirational; the studio OS at full expression. **Demonstrates:** the corporate blueprint, P-11, P-13, detach-and-reuse, KSVGPS-as-outsourced-department.
