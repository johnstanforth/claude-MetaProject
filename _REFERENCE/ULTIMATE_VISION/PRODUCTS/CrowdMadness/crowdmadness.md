## Venture origin & lineage — MobThought (2004–2008) → CrowdMadness  ⭐ *(the decade-arc centerpiece)*

**What we know / infer** *(per John, this session, unless cited otherwise)*
- **MobThought** was a 2004–2008 prediction-market **game** (sources: "online market-research startup, 2004–2008," `REVISED_ANALYSIS_UltimateVision.md` / `notes-clarifying-UltimateVisionGuide.md`). It **owned the CrowdMadness and CrowdResearch names/domains** then; `mobthought.com` was registered **2001-11-04**, `crowdresearch.com` **2008-02-26** (`DOMAIN_LIST.md`).
- **Explicitly a game, not gambling:** built with heavy fine-print — "*this is a GAME, giving prizes for winning bets / topping leaderboards; this is NOT a for-money prediction market*" — because a real-money prediction market **would have been illegal in the US in 2004–2008** *(per John)*.
- **The "background parallel thread" thesis:** the team expected US gambling/prediction-market law to evolve and intended to keep building, ready to launch when it did *(per John)*. It did change (the 2018 PASPA repeal → FanDuel/DraftKings → Polymarket/Kalshi + CFTC) — but **by 2018 life obstacles intervened** and they didn't execute *(per John)*.
- **The advisor bench was unusually senior** *(per John)*: **Bill Brand** (President, HSN.com), **Stephen McHenry** (Chancellor of Worldwide SRE, Google — 6 international offices), **Jonathan Forster** (first hire & VP Marketing, Spotify — offered intros to Daniel Ek). Worked alongside contacts at Fox Studios and other Hollywood studios/TV producers.
- **Why reboot NOW (the LLM-economics thesis)** *(per John)*: the regulatory environment is now *wildly* more complex (per-state + CFTC), which in 2005 would have meant **tens of millions in legal** and a dead business model — but **LLM agents can now track every state/federal regulatory change nightly and translate it into required app changes** for a few thousand in engineering. That inversion is the core reason CrowdMadness is feasible now. *(KSVGPS's own purpose: had MobThought been modeled in a KSVGPS-like system 10–20 years ago, the reboot would be assembling-from-a-living-model instead of from-scratch.)*
- **Modeling note:** MobThought→CrowdMadness is the **canonical `successor-of` / `historically-owned` example** in the KSVGPS entity-model research (`kingstrat-adventuregps/.../analysis-03--lineage-successor.md`: "MobThought *ended* 2008; CrowdMadness was a *later reboot*"; "**not** MobThought v2 — its own venture"). *(Erratum to preserve: one temporal-model example doc, `analysis-02`, uses dummy dates "2010–2013" for MobThought — **2004–2008 is authoritative**.)* This decade-arc-with-reboot is logged as `[Backlog:RESEARCH] R-003`.
