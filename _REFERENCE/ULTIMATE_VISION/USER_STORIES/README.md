# USER_STORIES — The Ecosystem in Motion

> Cross-product narrative scenarios — the stories no single repo can tell, because their whole point
> is what happens *between* products. These are how the vision becomes legible: one natural-language
> moment, traced as it moves through several products at once.

- **Created:** 2026-06-12 · Each story: the **narrative first** (with what's real-today vs aspirational marked), then **Ideation & Exploration** (variations and wild extensions).

## Why narrative scenarios matter here

The portfolio's defining bet is **discover-and-suggest from implicit real-world data** — observe what the user actually did; never make them fill out a form about themselves. That bet is invisible in any one product's feature list and obvious the moment you watch a single capture ripple across the ecosystem. These stories are the proof, the demo script, and the design forcing-function all at once.

A second through-line: the **human stays the executive; the agent is the clerk.** Across every story, agents *stage* proposals — they don't complete, delete, send, or commit on their own. (See the trust-boundary discussion in [LATER-002 §5.3](../../../_backlog_TODOs/LATER-002-recurring-autonomous-agent-tasks.md).)
