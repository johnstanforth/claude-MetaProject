## How to read these documents

Every doc here follows the same shape, by design:

1. **Consensus / known material first** — what the product or venture *is*, what's agreed, what's already decided or built. Traceable to the source repos.
2. **"Ideation & Exploration" last** — *every* speculative, "maybe," someday, or wildly-ambitious idea, captured in full and **committed to nothing.** This is the point: widen the considered space for later, lose no idea. Items are tagged by origin — a repo's backlog/research, or **✦ new this session** (dreamed up during consolidation, while the whole portfolio was in view at once).

Where the source repos contradict each other, the doc says so inline and links [`../ERRATA.md`](../ERRATA.md).

## The portfolio in one breath

Two corporate families plus a few independent ventures, ~24 repos, one shared philosophy — **discover-and-suggest from implicit real-world data rather than interrogating the user** — expressed in a dozen domains:

- **ExoDev / AIXO.Dev** — tools and a platform for **human + AI software teams** (the flagship `aixocode` TUI, the `aixodev-web` platform, a cloud of prototypes, a planned desktop edition).
- **Divia.AI** — a **personal-knowledge & life-organization ecosystem**: a desktop outliner (Professional), a team server (Enterprise), an agent backbone (Swarm), Gmail/mobile CRM viewers (DiviaContacts), an open household hub (DiviaHome), a mobile app (Divia.Life), a card-rendering layer (DiviaCards), all speaking the `.dvai` format and the Divia.Network integration layer, with a 501(c)(3) (Divia.Foundation) and a future identity SaaS (Divia.AI Global).
- **Independent / emerging** — **LegendaryMoney** (AI PFM, its own LLC), **KingStratVC Knowledgebase** (a PE/VC firm's intranet + Enterprise-client trial), **SattvasicHealth** (health aggregator), and **TastyPal** (TastyPantry + the standalone **spicemaster3000** flavor-tech project).
