# Idea — AI personal-finance manager (confidence-aware ledger)

> Layer-A Idea node (durable · brand-free). Model: [`STRATEGIC-LANDSCAPE-MODEL.md`](../../STRATEGIC-LANDSCAPE-MODEL.md). **Bootstrap seed.**

- **Topic(s):** [personal-finance](../TOPICS/personal-finance.md).
- **One-line:** An AI personal-finance manager built for messy, incomplete, real-world money — a confidence-aware ledger that **estimates rather than abstains**, captures in natural language, and reconciles from balance anchors ("Quicken for the rest of us").
