# Idea — 3D home capture & reconstruction

> Layer-A Idea node (durable · brand-free). Model: [`STRATEGIC-LANDSCAPE-MODEL.md`](../../STRATEGIC-LANDSCAPE-MODEL.md). **Bootstrap seed.**

- **Topic(s):** [Real Estate](../TOPICS/real-estate.md).
- **One-line:** A 3D virtual-home capture & reconstruction app (a Zillow-3D-Home equivalent) — a user walks a property capturing photos / AR frames and the app assembles a navigable interior 3D model, with measurements/sightlines as a rights-clean, first-party analytical input (not just a cinematic display).
- **Axes (Idea-level):** Conviction = **active** — a **dedicated long-term product, NOT a throwaway spike** (only its Phase 01 is spike-scoped, to de-risk camera/AR/depth/reconstruction) · Horizon = long-term (deliberately kept separate from the mobile app) · Provenance = FracRealHomes' need for first-party geometry/sightline inputs feeding valuation · Leverage = capture/AR/photogrammetry; output feeds the layered Unreal-Engine neighborhood model and the valuation engine's geometry/sightline inputs.
- **Idea↔Idea edges:** `enables`/feeds [view-durability risk modeling](view-durability-risk-modeling.md) and [AVM / property-estimation](avm-property-estimation.md) (geometry/sightlines as analytical inputs) · sibling-of [fractional-ownership co-ownership marketplace](fractional-ownership-co-ownership-marketplace.md) (FracRealHomes siblings; different capture-side audience — listing agents, real-estate photographers, sellers, field staff).
- **Channel (Idea→Venture, time-bounded):** the **FracRealHomes 3D Home** product within [FracRealHomes](../PRODUCTS/FracRealHomes/fracrealhomes.md) (scaffold, Phase 00 pending; proprietary/closed-source).
- **Realized by (Build Lines, Layer B):** the **3D-Capture Line** (`fracrealhomes-android`, native Kotlin/Android; "3D-Product" Build Envelope, long-term; capture/AR/photogrammetry firmed in Phase 01) — its own product's releases (scheme TBD, distinct from the EstimatePacket Version-Releases); feeds the others via a hand-off contract (Unreal/web), not a merge.
