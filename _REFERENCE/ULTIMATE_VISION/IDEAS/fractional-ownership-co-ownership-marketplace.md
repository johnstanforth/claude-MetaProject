# Idea — Fractional-ownership / co-ownership marketplace

> Layer-A Idea node (durable · brand-free). Model: [`STRATEGIC-LANDSCAPE-MODEL.md`](../../STRATEGIC-LANDSCAPE-MODEL.md). **Bootstrap seed.**

- **Topic(s):** [Real Estate](../TOPICS/real-estate.md).
- **One-line:** A Pacaso-style marketplace streamlining the creation, **co-purchase, and long-term multi-owner management** of fractional-ownership (e.g. 1/8-share) luxury residential properties — fusing Pacaso (co-ownership structuring), Airbnb (owner-use scheduling), and Asana (multi-owner maintenance/cost/tax coordination), with a **fractional value bridge** (whole-home → pro-rata → turnkey → calendar → reserves → view-risk → governance/liquidity → share fair value) made visible.
- **Axes (Idea-level):** Conviction = **active** (scaffold; Phase 00 pending) · Horizon = **v1 now** (a narrow, human-reviewed buyer-diligence packet for one launch market) · Provenance = John's real-estate venture thesis (the empirical "Pacaso 8× liquidity trap": whole-home value shown as exactly 8× the 1/8 share, yet the same home's resale shares list at different prices — a 1/8 share ≠ whole-home/8) · Leverage = the AVM valuation baseline; delegated financial services from LegendaryMoney over the Divia protocol.
- **Idea↔Idea edges:** sibling-of [AVM / property-estimation](avm-property-estimation.md) (same Topic, **different** Idea — co-ownership marketplace vs. valuation engine; the AVM is the valuation baseline this builds on) · sibling-of [view-durability risk modeling](view-durability-risk-modeling.md) and [3D home capture & reconstruction](3d-home-capture-reconstruction.md) (FracRealHomes siblings) · **consumes** [AI personal-finance manager](ai-personal-finance-manager.md) (delegates purchase flows / long-term operating-cost accounting / tax-implication handling to LegendaryMoney over the Divia protocol, rather than reimplementing them).
- **Channel (Idea→Venture, time-bounded):** [FracRealHomes](../PRODUCTS/FracRealHomes/fracrealhomes.md) (Fractional Reality Homes LLC; scaffold, Phase 00 pending; proprietary/closed-source). First market (beachhead): Manhattan Beach / South Bay LA.
- **Realized by (Build Lines, Layer B):** the **EstimatePacket Line** ("Seed"-class Python/Flask; delivers public v1.0→v4.0, the web + mobile surfaces — `fracrealhomes-web` + `fracrealhomes-flutter`); legal-classification gate (LLC interest / TIC / private-residence-club / securities-style) is a front-door gate before any fractional-share estimate. See [engineering brief](../../SOFTWARE_DEV/fracrealhomes.md).
