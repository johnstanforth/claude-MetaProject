# Idea — AR storefront-discovery (point-your-camera → federated-entity overlay)

> Layer-A Idea node (durable · brand-free). Model: [`STRATEGIC-LANDSCAPE-MODEL.md`](../../STRATEGIC-LANDSCAPE-MODEL.md). **Bootstrap seed.**

- **Topic(s):** [contacts-and-crm](../TOPICS/contacts-and-crm.md) (+ [federation-identity-network](../TOPICS/federation-identity-network.md))
- **One-line:** Point your phone camera at a street and it overlays the correct Divia.Network links on the stores you see — via GPS + compass + light OCR of store names — even for shops without a QR sticker; resolving physical Places to federated entities.
- **Axes (Idea-level):** Conviction = **aspirational** ("a later, fun feature") · Horizon = **later** (Phase-01-experimental on `diviacontacts-android`) · Provenance = the Divia-platform **federation research** in `kingstrat-adventuregps` (DIVIA_PLATFORM_VISION_AND_FEDERATION §5 + analysis-43 §4), consolidated for product-placement by [LATER-004 §4](../../../_backlog_TODOs/LATER-004-mobile-cross-platform-strategy.md) · Leverage = native camera/AR hardware (ARCore, Camera2, depth); the federated-identity registry; the `Divia.Network/{company}/{location}` shop-window-QR surface.
- **Idea↔Idea edges:** `sibling-of` [AI contacts / relationship manager](ai-contacts-relationship-manager.md) (same DiviaContacts job — resolving People/**Places**/Companies to entities — extended from inbox to physical world) · `depends-on` the federated-identity / Global-Registry idea (its research home is `kingstrat-adventuregps`, NOT a divia* repo — see Notes)
- **Channel (Idea→Venture, time-bounded):** [DiviaContacts](../PRODUCTS/DiviaAI/diviacontacts.md) (🟠 placeholder) — specifically `diviacontacts-android` as the **experimental AR substrate** (it already requires high-threshold Play permissions, so AR doesn't push it to a new extreme; putting AR in a Flutter messaging app like Divia.Life would). Divia.Life is a "maybe, if fun enough."
- **Realized by (Build Lines, Layer B):** TBD — native Kotlin `diviacontacts-android`; option-space lives in its `ROADMAP.md` as Phase-01-experimental.
- **Notes:** One of four identity-link surfaces (with `divia.me/{handle}`, the shop-window QR, the business-card QR). Technical grounding: dynamic QR (encodes a stable URL), GS1-GLN / Handle prefix-delegation, `did:web` + signed Verifiable Credentials with selective disclosure.
