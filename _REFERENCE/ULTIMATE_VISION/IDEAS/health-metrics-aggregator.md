# Idea — Health-metrics aggregator + correlation engine

> Layer-A Idea node (durable · brand-free). Model: [`STRATEGIC-LANDSCAPE-MODEL.md`](../../STRATEGIC-LANDSCAPE-MODEL.md). **Bootstrap seed.**

- **Topic(s):** [health-and-wellness](../TOPICS/health-and-wellness.md).
- **One-line:** A personal health-metrics aggregator that unifies a lifetime of scattered health data — labs, CGM, weight/DEXA, Rx/supplements, calories/macros — into one longitudinal record, then graph → trend → (future) **correlate** (e.g. certain foods → headaches ~2 hours later).
- **Axes (Idea-level):** Conviction = **active** (Phase 00 pending; personal-aggregator scope) · Horizon = **v1 = one metric domain end-to-end** (e.g. a cross-lab analyte timeline) now; the correlation engine is the future payoff · Provenance = the portfolio's "implicit-data, discover-and-suggest" promise applied to health · Leverage = an import-adapter/dedup architecture for legacy/device exports; the **time-decay refill-runway primitive** shared with finance scheduled-outflows and home grocery replenishment ("build it once, reuse across all three").
