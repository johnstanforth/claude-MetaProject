### ② John — pushback on the audience

> *"I want to push back on one part of this: 'technical builders (looking for code blocks, deployment blueprints, infrastructure)' — that's the wrong audience…"*

The point: `kingstrat-venturegps` is **not** a developer tool. Its users are the firm's **internal C-suite / operators / studio leadership** and its **LP investors** — a private-equity / venture-studio context. Any "deep-tech" styling should serve *impression and authority*, not literal coding.

### ③ Gemini — refit: a dual-audience "Strategic Operating System"

Reframes the product as the **Strategic Operating System of Kingmaker Strategic Venture Partners LLC**, with a **Dual-Node Architecture** for its two audiences:

- **Studio Node (Internal Operators)** — the platform's day-to-day operational/management home for partners, operators, and venture architects.
- **Investor Node (External Capital)** — LPs get a bird's-eye, performance-visibility view; they don't run active management.

Both view the business top-down; one is for *operating*, one is for *performance visibility*.

### ④ John — the GPS / vector-tracking-loop idea

> *"What about a name that evokes something more like GPS navigation, or maybe Vector-based tracking loops that combine the two tasks of signal tracking and position/velocity estimation into one algorithm?"*

### ⑤ Gemini — the Vector Tracking Loop (VTL) analogy + the navigation names

Calls it a *"brilliant mechanical analogy."* In signal processing, a **Vector Tracking Loop** is exactly that: instead of tracking each satellite signal in an **isolated silo**, a vector loop processes **all channels simultaneously** through a **centralized estimation engine** (an Extended Kalman Filter). Map onto the platform:

> Instead of treating portfolio companies as isolated vacuums, the platform **aggregates all operational signals, capital efficiency, and strategic velocity** and processes them through one centralized estimation engine. If a single startup hits friction/"noise," the **collective strength, shared infrastructure, and playbooks of the broader Kingmaker studio are dynamically leveraged to keep the entire fund's lock on target.**

Candidate names: **KingStrat VentureGPS**, **KingStrat VTL** (Project VTL), **KingStrat Ephemeris** (Project Ephemeris).

### ⑥ John — go with VentureGPS; map the architecture

Leans **`KingStrat VentureGPS`**. (The welcome / "About the System" note can carry the VTL explanation.) Asks Gemini to lay out the data layout and modules.
