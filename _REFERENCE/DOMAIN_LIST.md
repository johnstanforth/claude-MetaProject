> A flat registry of every **owned/registered** domain in the portfolio, **sorted alphabetically by domain**.
> The hierarchical company → product → redirect structure (and the strategic `BUY-DOMAIN` targets) lives
> in [`DOMAIN_MAPPINGS.md`](DOMAIN_MAPPINGS.md); this file is the lookup table.
>
> - **Creation Date** is the original registration date from RDAP, expressed in **UTC** — so it can differ
>   by one calendar day from a registrar panel's local-time display (the same UTC offset explains why a few
>   recorded Expiration Dates sit one day before RDAP's). Full raw RDAP responses are cached in `domains_rdap.jsonl`.
> - **Expiration Date** values are as recorded from the registrar (John-verified); not overwritten by RDAP.
> - **Creation Date `—`** = the TLD has no public RDAP server (`.me`, `.sh`, `.vc`); not yet backfillable.
> - Registrars: **Spaceship.com** (most) and **Namecheap.com** (a few). `BUY-DOMAIN` targets are excluded.
> - Subdomains (e.g. `dallas.exodev.pro`) are not separate registrations and are excluded.
> - `†` marks a domain slated for deprecation/deletion (see `DOMAIN_MAPPINGS.md`).
